ADDISON PERRY

OBJECTS ARCHITECTURE ABOUT

Addison Perry is an architecture student and designer interested in objects, spatial systems, and the social life of buildings.

His work explores how architecture can operate as a cultural container, foregrounding community narratives, material presence, and everyday use.

He is currently completing his undergraduate architecture degree at Cal Poly Pomona and works with Modern for All, focusing on furniture, archives, and restoration.
